IT-Related Ethics

In ever evolving world of Information Technology, ethics help maintain our sense of morality. Below are some of the ethics for an IT professional found within the Association for Computing Machinery's Code of Ethics:

  • An IT professional should seek to improve quality of life of people and improve society
  • An IT professional be fair, just, and seek to do no harm to everyone
  • An IT professional should respect privacy and uphold confidentiality
  • An IT professional should respect patents, copyrights, licensing agreements, etc.
